# Project Overview

In this project, you'll Lead AI Architect at a data intelligence firm. Your clients are high-level executives who need deep, accurate technical briefings - and they need them fast.

Your VP of Engineering has just put a new ticket on your desk: **Project DeepDive**.

**The Goal**: Build an Agentic AI Research Assistant. When asked a hard question, it shouldn't just guess. It should:

1. **Plan** where to look.
2. **Go read** actual papers and websites (simulated tools in this project).
3. **Draft** an answer.
4. **Critique** its own work (and fix it if it doesn't meet criteria).
5. **Fact-check** the claims.
6. **Format** the bibliography.

You'll use Google ADK (Agent Development Kit) to implement a research team of agents to output a research report.

## Your Mission

You're provided with a `project-starter` codebase. The "infrastructure" (the boilerplate code) is there, but the agent implementation is missing.

You will see `TODO` markers in the codebase. Your job is to inject ADK and agentic workflow patterns to make the research assistant work.
